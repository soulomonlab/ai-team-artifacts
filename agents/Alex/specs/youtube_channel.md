# Feature: Launch Official YouTube Channel
**Goal:** Establish a branded YouTube presence to increase product awareness and funnel users to the app.
**North Star Impact:** Increase user acquisition and engagement through organic video discovery and channel-driven traffic.
**Users:** Prospective users researching our product; existing users who want tutorials, release notes, and case studies.
**RICE Score:** Reach=50,000 × Impact=2 × Confidence=70% / Effort=2w = 35,000
**Kano Category:** Performance

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- [ ] YouTube channel created with verified brand name and consistent handle
- [ ] Channel art (banner + avatar) approved and uploaded
- [ ] Channel About section filled with SEO-optimized description and links
- [ ] Upload workflow documented (templates for title, description, tags, thumbnails)
- [ ] At least 5 baseline videos uploaded: 1 product intro, 2 feature tutorials, 1 customer story, 1 release highlights
- [ ] Thumbnails and captions (closed captions) added for all baseline videos
- [ ] Tracking in analytics configured (UTM templates, campaign tracking, and dashboard)
- [ ] Launch promotion plan with channels and KPIs drafted

**Out of Scope:**
- Full video production (scripting and editing will be coordinated but not executed in this PRD)
- Ongoing content calendar beyond baseline launch

**Success Metrics:**
- Subscribers: 5,000 within 3 months
- Total views: 50,000 within 3 months
- Average view duration: > 40% of video length
- Click-through to site/app from video links: > 1.5% CTR
