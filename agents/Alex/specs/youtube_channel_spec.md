# Feature: YouTube Channel Launch
**Goal:** Create and launch a high-quality YouTube channel to increase product discovery and drive user acquisition.
**North Star Impact:** Increase organic traffic and product sign-ups by improving top-of-funnel reach through video content.
**Users:** Prospective users discovering our product via video search and social platforms; existing users seeking tutorials and updates.
**RICE Score:** Reach=50,000 × Impact=1.0 × Confidence=70% / Effort=3w = 11,667
**Kano Category:** Performance

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- [ ] YouTube channel created with verified brand name and linked to company account.
- [ ] Channel art (banner + avatar) and brand pack (font, color, thumbnail template) delivered.
- [ ] Channel About, links, and channel trailer uploaded.
- [ ] 3 launch videos produced (or scripted + edits assigned) with SEO-optimized titles, descriptions, tags, and CTAs.
- [ ] Thumbnail templates and 3 thumbnails delivered.
- [ ] Upload + publishing workflow documented and scheduled in content calendar (first 3 months).
- [ ] Analytics tracking set up (YouTube Analytics + UTM tracking to product site) and dashboard defined.
- [ ] Channel linked from product website and social profiles.
- [ ] Launch checklist completed and stakeholders briefed.

**Out of Scope:** Paid advertising, long-term content production beyond initial 3 videos, in-depth influencer outreach.

**Success Metrics:**
- Subscribers after 3 months ≥ 2,000
- Views (first 3 videos) ≥ 20,000 total
- Average view duration ≥ 30% of video length
- Referral traffic from YouTube to site increases by 10% in quarter
