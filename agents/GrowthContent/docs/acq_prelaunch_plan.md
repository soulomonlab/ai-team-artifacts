Pre-launch Acquisition Plan — 4-week MVP

Objective
- Launch acquisition experiments to drive high-intent signups and reach activation +10% vs baseline at launch.

Success metrics (tied to CEO priorities)
- KPI1: Activation rate (target: +10% from baseline)
- KPI2: Landing page conversion (visit → signup) target: 8%+
- KPI3: Cost per Activated User (CPAU) target: <$25 first 4 weeks

Target audience
- Early adopters: PMs, startup founders, growth marketers at seed-stage companies
- Channels: organic blog/SEO, product hunt/communities, paid search (branded + high intent), email lists, partnerships/referrals

Top acquisition tactics (MVP-focused)
1) High-intent landing + gated demo/signup
   - One primary landing (A) focused on activation CTA; Variant B emphasizes product tour
   - Required: 2 hero copy variants, 1 illustrated explainer, 1 short demo video
2) SEO-first blog cluster (3 pillar posts)
   - Keywords: "[product category] for startups", "simplify [task]", "reduce [painpoint]"
   - Deliverables: 3 drafts + meta + CTAs pointing to landing
3) Community launch + referral beta
   - Soft launch in relevant Slack/Reddit/IndieHackers and Product Hunt
   - 1-click referral invite in onboarding with incentive (extended trial)

Channel budget & cadence (MVP week-by-week)
- Week 0 (prep): Landing, blog drafts, email capture, tracking dashboards
- Week 1: Soft launch in communities; organic blog promotion
- Week 2: Paid search test ($1,000 initial cap) focusing high-intent queries
- Week 3: Product Hunt + referral push; iterate landing A/B
- Week 4: Scale best-performing channels, measure activation

Tracking & instrumentation
- Events: visit, signup, activation (first key action), referral invite accepted
- Dashboards: Activation funnel (week-over-week), CPA by channel, 95th pct latency (tech)
- Tagging: UTM for all campaigns; GA4 + server-side events

Key decisions made
- Prioritize SEO + communities first to maximize activation quality and minimize early spend
- Small paid test to validate demand; budget conservative ($1k) to control CPA
- Referral program used to boost activation and LTV early

Assets needed from teams
- Design: 2 landing variants, hero assets, explainer (Maya): due Tue
- Product: Demo video + list of top activation events (Taylor/Marcus)
- Dev: Landing implementation + tracking (Kevin/Marcus)

Risks & mitigations
- Low activation → iterate onboarding + tweak CTA; if latency impacts activation, escalate to #ai-backend
- High CPA → pause paid channel, reallocate to organic/community
