Prelaunch Acquisition Plan — v1

Executive summary
- Goal: Build a high-intent lead funnel for MVP launch (end of Q2). Run two parallel, testable channels for 2–4 weeks to validate acquisition cost, conversion funnel, and messaging.
- Primary owner: Jessica (Growth). Draft created by GrowthContent. Next: finalize creatives, tracking, and launch tests.

Target audience
- Early adopters with immediate pain X (describe product-specific persona). Prioritize paid intent (search) and educational content audiences.

Top 2 testable channels (P0 tests)
1) Paid Search (Google Search / Microsoft Ads)
   - Why: Captures high purchase intent; fastest signal for CPA and conversion rate.
   - Tactical plan:
     - Keywords: high-intent and competitor + problem keywords (shortlist to be finalized by Jessica).
     - Ads: 3 headline variants x 2 descriptions. Direct CTA: "Get early access" / "Start free trial".
     - Landing: Dedicated search landing page with 3 copy variants (see sample headlines below), single CTA, social proof placeholder, 1-step signup (email + country).
   - Budget & KPI (2-week test): $2k–$5k spend; target CPL < $30; conversion rate on landing > 8% (benchmark for high-intent).
   - Tracking: Google Ads, GA4, conversion pixel, UTM naming conventions, LTV-enabled events.

2) Content SEO + Gated Guide (Organic + Email Nurture)
   - Why: Lower CPM, builds long-term funnel and LTV; content attracts qualified leads searching for solution-related topics.
   - Tactical plan:
     - Publish 1 long-form cornerstone article + 3 supporting posts (SEO cluster) linked to a gated "MVP Playbook" (PDF).
     - Landing for guide with email capture + 2-step micro-commitment (email → short qual question optional).
     - Drip: 3-email nurture sequence (value + social proof + CTA to product waitlist).
   - Budget & KPI (4-week baseline): Content production cost $1k–$2k; target conversion to email list 5–10% on article-to-gate flows; engaged lead to product signup 10% of gated leads.
   - Tracking: GA4, UTM, email platform metrics, organic keyword rank tracking.

Sample landing copy variants (search landing)
- Variant A (Direct—High intent)
  Headline: "Get [Outcome] Today — Join MVP Early Access"
  Subhead: "Verify results in minutes. No credit card."

- Variant B (Problem-led)
  Headline: "Tired of [Pain]? Try the easiest way to [Outcome]"
  Subhead: "Early access — limited seats. Join the waitlist."

- Variant C (Social-proof)
  Headline: "Trusted by early teams to [Outcome]"
  Subhead: "See real results in X days — sign up for early access."

Creative & assets required
- 3 landing copy variants + 3 search ad creatives
- 1 hero image + 2 thumbnail variations
- Gated PDF guide (short, 6–8 pages) and 4 blog posts
- Email drip sequence (3 emails) and signup confirmation page

Measurement & acceptance criteria (MVP gating)
- Channel A (Paid Search): CPL validated < target ($30) OR conversion rate > 8% with stable CPCs after 2 weeks.
- Channel B (Content): Organic traffic growth to cornerstone article to >200 sessions/week and gated conversion rate >=5% by week 4.
- Baseline tracking: GA4 + Ads + Email platform connected; UTM taxonomy standardized.

Risks & mitigations
- Low traffic for SEO in 4 weeks → run lightweight paid social retargeting to article viewers.
- Landing copy untested → run A/B on headlines first 48–72 hours and allocate budget to winning variant.
